02

Nature of Relationship

Agent is an independent contractor, not an employee of POST205. This agreement does not create an employer-employee relationship. Agent has full autonomy over methods, schedule, and approach. POST205 specifies only the services to be referred and the commission structure. Agent is not subject to POST205 employment policies and is not entitled to any employee benefits, including but not limited to 13th-month pay, leave credits, or social security contributions from POST205.

03

Scope of Engagement

Agent's role is limited to referral and introduction. Agent identifies prospective clients and introduces them to POST205. POST205 manages all communication, pitching, proposal preparation, negotiation, closing, and project delivery. Agent is copied on relevant communications as a courtesy. No sales work, project management, client servicing, or delivery work is expected from Agent.

04

05

What Does Not Earn Commission

Commission is not earned on:

  • Monthly retainer fees, subscription fees, or any recurring charges, regardless of client origin
  • Deals where Agent is not the originating contact
  • Referrals that POST205 declines to pursue
  • Projects above PHP 500,000 without a separately agreed commission in writing

New projects from a referred client do earn commission at the same rate and structure as the original project.

06

Payment Schedule

Commissions are calculated monthly. Payment is released within 10 business days of the end of each calendar month, covering all commissions earned in that month. Payment is made via bank transfer to Agent's registered account. POST205 will provide a written commission statement with each payment.

07

Tax and Withholding

POST205 will withhold Expanded Withholding Tax (EWT) at the rate of ten percent (10%) on all commission payments, as required under the National Internal Revenue Code and BIR regulations for individual independent contractors. This rate applies provided Agent submits a Sworn Declaration of Gross Receipts by January 15 of each applicable year; failure to submit will result in the applicable statutory rate. POST205 will issue BIR Form 2307 (Certificate of Creditable Tax Withheld at Source) to Agent on a quarterly basis. Agent is responsible for filing and paying all income taxes, SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG contributions from their own earnings. Agent must provide a valid BIR Tax Identification Number (TIN) before receiving any commission payment.

08

Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

Definition. "Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by POST205 to Agent in connection with this Agreement, including but not limited to: pricing, rates, and fee structures; proposal and contract content; service methods, workflows, and technical approaches; financial data and business strategy; and any information that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of the disclosure. For the avoidance of doubt, client identities and contacts introduced by Agent to POST205 remain Agent's confidential information, and POST205 shall not disclose Agent's identity as the source of any referral without Agent's prior written consent.

Exclusions. Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no act or omission of Agent; (b) was already in Agent's possession prior to disclosure, as evidenced by contemporaneous written records; (c) is lawfully received by Agent from a third party without restriction; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order, provided that Agent gives POST205 prompt prior written notice and cooperates in seeking a protective order.

Obligations. Agent agrees to: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without POST205's prior written consent; (c) use Confidential Information solely to perform the referral activities contemplated by this Agreement; and (d) protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect Agent's own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care.

Survival and Return. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years. Upon written request by POST205, Agent shall promptly return or certifiably destroy all Confidential Information in Agent's possession.

Remedies. Agent acknowledges that any breach or threatened breach of this clause may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate, and that POST205 shall be entitled to seek injunctive or other equitable relief without the requirement of posting bond or other security, in addition to all other remedies available at law or in equity.

09

Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, POST205 will pay all earned and unpaid commissions within 30 days.

Tail period: If Agent referred a client before the termination date and a project closes and clears payment within 60 days of termination, Agent earns commission on that project. Projects that do not close and clear within 60 days of termination do not earn commission.

POST205 may decline to pursue any referred client at any time without owing commission.

10

Independence

This agreement does not prevent Agent from pursuing other professional opportunities. Agent may not represent directly competing consultancies while this agreement is active. Agent acknowledges that POST205 may engage other referral agents simultaneously.

11

Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of the Philippines. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Quezon City, Metro Manila.

12

Entire Agreement

This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties on this subject. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
