POST205 Intelligence

Every morning, your team opens the admin portal to a summary of the previous day across all parks: check-ins, ticket sales, anything that looked unusual. It is already there.

Throughout the day the platform watches quietly. A park down 40% on a Saturday compared to last month. A ticket type that stopped selling after a price change. These get surfaced before they become a problem.

Guest profiles are scored in the background by visit frequency, spend, parks visited, and recency. When the team runs a campaign, the scoring is already there. After each campaign, a short recap shows delivery, reach, and suggested timing for the next one.

The SMS composer includes a writing assistant. Describe the offer, the park, and the tone. A draft message comes back ready to review and send.

Included in the monthly retainer. No separate activation required.
