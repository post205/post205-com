Component 06

Sending SMS to your guests.

Today

Zero SMS. No direct line to guests after purchase.

With POST205

Automated confirmations and reminders. Send campaigns to any park's guest list.

Every guest who buys a ticket leaves a phone number. That number goes into the contact list for that park automatically. Booking confirmations and day-before reminders send on their own — no one needs to do anything. When your team wants to send a promo or announcement, there's a tool in the admin portal: pick the group, write the message, send.

Before any campaign goes out, the list gets cleaned: duplicates removed, people who opted out excluded, anyone messaged too recently held back. Messages go out under the ZOOMANITY name. Each message includes a number guests can text back if they have questions.

POST205 Intelligence

Every morning, the admin portal opens to a summary of the day before: check-ins, ticket sales, anything that stood out. Your team doesn't have to pull reports — it's already on the screen.

The system also flags things you might not catch otherwise. A park that's 40% down on a Saturday compared to last month. A ticket type that stopped selling after a price change. These show up in the portal before they become a problem.

Guest profiles are ranked quietly in the background — by how often they visit, how much they spend, which parks, and how recently. When your team runs a campaign, that ranking is already there to use. After each campaign, a short report shows how many were reached and when to send the next one.

The SMS composer has a writing assistant built in. Describe the promo, the park, and the tone. A draft comes back ready to review and send.
