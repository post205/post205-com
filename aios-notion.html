AIOS · Notion

An AI operating system for your Notion

An AI Operating System (AIOS) turns Claude into a working part of your business, not a chatbot you ask one-off questions. It learns how you operate, connects to the tools you already use, and does real work for you: finding information, drafting, updating records, even running tasks while you're away. This is the version built for Notion. Point it at your workspace, and Claude can read and write your Notion for you. Below is everything in the kit.
What you'll need
Three things to set up first. The kit walks you through the rest.
A paid Claude account required
Claude is the AI that does the work. It runs on a paid plan (Claude Pro or Max) or on API billing. The free tier won't run it, so a paid account is the one real cost here.
VS Code free
The free editor Claude works inside. Install it once, then open the kit in it.
A Notion account free or paid
Whatever plan you already have. You create a free integration token and share the pages you want Claude to reach. Two minutes, and the steps are in the kit.

The two frameworks

The Shift how you think

Why no MCP

An MCP loads its whole toolset into every conversation, whether you use it or not. You pay that token cost up front, every time. The Notion API is small, so the kit skips the MCP and calls it directly with curl. You pay for what you use, nothing more. It's cheaper to run, and it's yours.
